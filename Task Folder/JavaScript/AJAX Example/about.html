About Great Wall of China

Holding the title of “longest human-made structure in the world,” the Great Wall is actually many walls running through northern China and southern Mongolia. One significant stretch is particularly well preserved and is the part that most of us visit today.

About Great Pyramid of Giza

Located in Egypt, the Great Pyramid is the only original wonder still standing. The largest of the three Pyramids of Giza, the Great Pyramid held the distinction of being one of the tallest buildings ever built for nearly 4,000 years.
